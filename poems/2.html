Nostalgia

by Anonymous

Faded photos,
Time goes back,

Childhood laughter,
Windowsill dreams.

Old tree swings,
Whispers soft,

Summer nights,
Fireflies dance.

Gentle rain,
Pavement shines,

Worn-out books,
Tales unwind.

Songs remembered,
Voices blend,

Familiar streets,
Paths we walked.

Echoes linger,
Moments freeze,

Scent of pine,
Warm embraces,

Rivers flow,
Footsteps trace,

Remember time,
Its fast pace.
