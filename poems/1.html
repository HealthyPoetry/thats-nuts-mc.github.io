Whispers in twilight

by Anonymous

Whispers in twilight,
Stars softly blink,
Shadows dance gently,
Moonlight’s sweet ink.

Leaves rustle low,
Crickets begin,
Night wraps around,
Dreams draw us in.

Cool breeze sighs,
Clouds drift away,
Heartbeats echo,
In night’s ballet.

Hope flickers bright,
Memories unfold,
Time stands still,
In stories told.

Dreams intertwine,
With whispers of fate,
A tapestry woven,
In moments we create.

Embers of warmth,
Silently embrace,
Under the sky,
We find our place.
